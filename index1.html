Phase 1: THE MAZE
During this part of the game, you will control a single unit (player 1 = blue, player 2 = white, player 3 = red, player 4 = gray), moving it with the “w, a, s, and d” keys (forward, left, down and right respectively). The goal is to collect as many gold icons (yellow) as possible within the allotted time of 2 minutes by navigating through the maze. Units can stand on top of each other and they cannot go through the walls of the maze. A base amount of ??? gold is given to you at the start of this phase, and the more gold you get, the more units you will be able to purchase for combat later in the game.

Phase 2: THE STORE
During this part of the game, you may purchase units to use in combat by spending the gold you got from phase 1. A total of 8 units may be purchased, and are purchased by double clicking on the unit’s row in the scroll bar. Should you be unhappy with any selection you make, you may reset your selections by clicking the reset button, giving you all your gold back and making it so you have 0 units selected. Be careful though, this part of the game is also timed and you are given 2 minutes to determine what you will use for combat (note: a player is given a goblin at the start of the next phase if they do not purchase any units). When you are finished making your purchases, click the ready button, or wait for time to expire. Nothing will happen until everyone has either clicked ready, or 2 minutes has passed. Your current and personal maximum resources are also displayed on this screen. The units that you can choose from are listed below.

Phase 3: THE BATTLEFIELD
This is where all the battling takes place, and in order to be victorious, you must be the last player on the map with units remaining. First, you must place the units in the order in which you purchased them by left clicking on empty tiles in your starting zone (Player 1 = bottom left, Player 2 = upper right, Player 3 = upper left, Player 4 = lower right). Once all your units have been placed, the player with the lowest gathered resources begins the game by left clicking a single unit and then clicking again somewhere on the game board. If the player right clicks on an opponent’s unit’s space, then an attack will be made against that unit. If that enemy’s unit was adjacent to your attacking unit, (left, right, forward, or backwards, not diagonally) then that enemy’s unit will retaliate, dealing damage to the attacking unit equal to 50% of the enemy unit’s attack/damage (warrior retaliates for 2 damage, rogue retaliates for 2 damage). If the enemy’s unit is not adjacent to your attacking unit, then no retaliation will occur (0 damage dealt to the attacker). If that “somewhere on the game board” left click is on an open space, and your unit’s movement/speed is greater than or equal to the number of squares required to reach that spot, your unit will move to that location. Units may move through other friendly units while moving and may attack through units in order to hit opponents units. After moving your unit, you may then make an attack with that unit (note: you do not have to move in order to make an attack). You will also see an information box on the right hand side of your screen during this phase. This information box contains the left clicked unit’s information, and also contains a button in order to end your turn (End Turn).
